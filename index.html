I am a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University, advised by Professor Jun Won Choi.

I obtained my M.S. advised by Professor Jun Won Choi at Hanyang University, and received my B.S. in Automotive Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from Kookmin University.
